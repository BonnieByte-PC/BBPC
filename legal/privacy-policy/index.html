Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 November 2025

1. Overview

At BonnieByte PC, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address (only when voluntarily provided)
  • Purchase information for support/warranty purposes
  • Technical data such as browser type, device, and IP address
  • Website usage analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing customer support and warranty services
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Sending optional updates (only if you subscribed)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK & EU data protection law, our legal bases include consent, legitimate interest, and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party platforms such as Amazon, PayPal, and analytics providers. These services have their own privacy practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for support, warranty, legal compliance, or subscription management.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and UK law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to data processing based on legitimate interest

8. Cookies

For information on how cookies are used on our website, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please email: hello@bonniebytepc.com
