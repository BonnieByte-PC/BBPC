Returns & Refund Policy

Last updated: 25 November 2025

1. Introduction

At BonnieByte PC, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. This policy explains how returns, refunds, and replacements work.

2. 30-Day Return Window

You may return your product within 30 days of delivery for a refund or replacement, provided the item is in good condition and includes all accessories.

3. Return Conditions

  • All returned items must be in good condition.
  • All included accessories (cables, screws, documentation) must be returned.
  • Products damaged by misuse or modification may not be eligible.

4. Refunds

Once the returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you of approval or rejection. Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method.

5. Replacements

If your item is faulty, we will send a replacement as quickly as possible after confirming the issue.

6. How to Start a Return

Please contact us at: returns@bonniebytepc.com

7. Amazon Purchases

If you purchased through Amazon, please start your return through Amazon's return system.

8. Contact

For return-related questions, email: returns@bonniebytepc.com
