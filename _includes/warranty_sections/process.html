How to Submit a Warranty Claim

To get started, contact our warranty team with a brief description of the issue. We usually reply within 1–2 business days.

What to include:

  • Your order number or proof of purchase
  • The affected product and quantity
  • A clear description of the issue
  • Photos or a short video (very helpful for fast assessment)

Once we’ve reviewed your case, we’ll guide you through the next steps.
