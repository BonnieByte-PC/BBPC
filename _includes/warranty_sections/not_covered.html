What This Warranty Does Not Cover

To keep things fair and consistent, the warranty does not cover damage resulting from improper use, modification, or circumstances outside normal operation.

  • Incorrect installation or wiring (e.g., plugging 5V ARGB into 12V RGB headers)
  • Physical damage, accidental damage, or cosmetic wear
  • Damage caused by liquid exposure, smoke, or fire
  • Running products outside their intended electrical or thermal limits
  • Third-party modifications or repairs
  • Damage caused by another component in your system

If you're unsure whether an issue is covered, feel free to ask — we’re always happy to take a look.
