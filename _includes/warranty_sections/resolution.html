7. What We Will Do

If your issue is covered under this warranty, we will take one of the following actions:

Component Replacement (Most Common)

If only one fan, cable, or the controller hub is faulty, we will replace that individual component.

This approach is faster, reduces unnecessary waste, and avoids returning a fully working bundle.

Multiple Component Failure

If two or more components from the same kit fail, we may replace the entire Northern Wind 120 bundle.
